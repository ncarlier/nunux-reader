NUNUX :: Reader

The super-fast-minimalist-nosql-opensource Google Reader revival.

  • Faster than light

    Thanks to Redis, Node.js and AngularJS

  • Responsive

    Work well on your phone, your tablet or your computer. With Touch support!

  • Free & open source

    This software free and under the GPLv3 licence.

Features

  • Manage your subscriptions

    Import/Export your subscriptions from/to another RSS reader thanks to OPML standard.

  • Touch

    View articles on your tablet or your mobile by swipe gesture.

  • Keyboard shortcuts

    Use keyboard shortcuts to be more efficient. Type '?' and see the magic appen.

  • Reactive

    Instantly get new published articles thanks to PubSubHubbub and a powerful and reactive aggregator daemon.

  • Save articles in the Cloud

    Uses external archive providers like NUNUX Keeper, Pocket or Dropbox.

  • Clean API

    Create your own client or service thanks to a complete RESTFul JSON API.

  • 100% Javascript

    One language to rule them all!
